## Interpretation

- **Pairwise distance (PW dist)**: mean absolute difference in H-H bond length
  between all pairs of selected frames. Higher = more diverse.
- **Uncertainty top-K** selects highest force_dev_max frames, which may cluster
  at similar bond lengths (low PW dist).
- **Diversity (FPS)** spreads selections across the bond-length range,
  giving higher PW dist at the cost of slightly lower average FDM.
- **Trust-level** selects from the candidate region (intermediate uncertainty),
  which may naturally cover a different bond-length range.

## Limitations

- Toy H2: only 1 degree of freedom (H-H distance). Real systems need richer descriptors.
- Single round comparison; multi-round diversity evolution not yet analyzed.
